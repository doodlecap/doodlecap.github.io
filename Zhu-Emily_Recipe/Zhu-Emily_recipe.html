Salted Hard Boiled Egg

A good, cheap snack.

Ingredients

  • egg
  • salt

Directions

  1. Submerge an egg in boiling water in a pot and leave it for 15 minutes.
  2. Turn off the fire and take the egg(s) out of the pot and into a bowl.
  3. Break the shell of the egg and liberally salt the hard boiled egg.
